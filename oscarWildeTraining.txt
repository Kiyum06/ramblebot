
Winter Night by the Fire

The winter wind is cold and light,
It dances softly through the night.
The stars above shine clear and bright,
While snowflakes sparkle in the moonlight.

I sit beside a fire of wood,
Its warmth feels gentle, calm, and good.
The flames rise high in orange glow,
While icy winds continue to blow.

The crackling logs sing quiet songs,
And peaceful moments drift along.
A mug of cocoa in my hand,
I watch the sleeping snowy land.

The night is still, the air is clear,
A season that I hold so dear.
Wrapped in a blanket, warm and tight,
I enjoy the winter's peaceful night.

The fire flickers, red and gold,
Keeping away the winter cold.
Beneath the stars, I sit and see,
How calm and beautiful life can be.

The snow keeps falling, soft and slow,
Covering fields in silver glow.
Beside the fire, I rest awhile,
And greet the night with a gentle smile.
